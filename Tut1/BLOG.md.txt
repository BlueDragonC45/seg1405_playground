There are many different meanings for the abbreviation ‘pm’ in software, like project manager, product manager, product owner, but really, they all come down to one thing. People Management. [1] In order to get a project up and running, and to get it out the door, you need the people that work on it to be organized, kept up to date, and working with other teams. This takes people skills, something not everyone can do. It takes knowing how to work with your people, and the best ways to get what you need from the other teams. All in all, managing a project, or a product, or even being the prime minister (PM), all takes the most important pm, people management.
